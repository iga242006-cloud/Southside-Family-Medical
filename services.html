Core Services

What we offer every patient.

Family Medicine for Every Age

From a newborn's first well-child visit to managing chronic conditions in a senior patient, Southside Family Medical Group provides the full spectrum of primary care — delivered by physicians who know your family's history.

We believe the best medicine is continuous medicine. When your physician has seen you through multiple phases of life, every appointment starts with context — not a blank page.

  • Annual wellness exams and physicals
  • Acute illness and same-week sick visits
  • Chronic disease management (diabetes, hypertension, thyroid, etc.)
  • Pediatric and adolescent care
  • Geriatric and senior care
  • Immunizations and preventive screenings
🧪

On-Site Laboratory Testing

When your physician needs blood work or lab results to guide your care, we handle it right here in the office. No referral to a separate lab facility, no extra trip across town, no delay waiting for records to transfer.

On-site lab capability means faster results, faster decisions, and care that doesn't lose momentum between appointments.

  • Blood draws and CBC panels
  • Metabolic and lipid panels
  • Urinalysis
  • Diabetes monitoring (A1C)
  • Thyroid function testing
  • Rapid strep and flu tests
❤️

Electrocardiogram (EKG / ECG)

Cardiac screening available directly in our office. When your physician needs a closer look at your heart rhythm or electrical activity, you don't have to be sent elsewhere — we can perform the EKG on the spot.

On-site EKG testing is part of our commitment to reducing the inconvenience that comes with fragmented healthcare — keeping your care coordinated and moving.

  • Resting 12-lead EKG
  • Cardiac screening for new patients and annual visits
  • Pre-operative clearance evaluations
  • Arrhythmia and palpitation workup support
