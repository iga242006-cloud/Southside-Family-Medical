Dr. Cynthia Williams

MD · Board-Certified Family Medicine

Dr. Cynthia Williams is a board-certified family medicine physician with a deep commitment to comprehensive, relationship-centered care. She brings both clinical rigor and genuine warmth to every patient encounter — whether it's a wellness exam for a young child or managing a long-term chronic condition for an adult patient.

A cornerstone of Southside Family Medical Group since its founding, Dr. Cynthia believes that knowing a patient's history — and their family's history — is foundational to delivering care that makes a real difference. Her approach is unhurried, thorough, and centered on the patient in front of her.

Dr. David Williams

MD · Board-Certified Family Medicine

Dr. David Williams is a board-certified family medicine physician with roots in Indianapolis's south side community. He practices evidence-based medicine with a pragmatic, patient-first approach — taking the time to understand not just a diagnosis, but the whole person behind it.

Dr. David has built lasting relationships with patients and families across south Indianapolis over many years of practice. Whether he's evaluating a new symptom, reviewing a complex medication regimen, or providing preventive guidance, he brings the same attentiveness and care to every visit.
